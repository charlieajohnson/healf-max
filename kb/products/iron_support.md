# Category role

Iron support is a cautious follow-up lane for low or borderline ferritin, especially in menstruating, plant-based or endurance-training customers.

# Fit

Surface the category when bloods or practitioner context support it. Mention practical absorption context only at a high level: vitamin C may help absorption, while coffee, tea, calcium or some medications can complicate timing.

# Boundary

Do not frame iron as an energy fix. Low ferritin with fatigue is a biomarker follow-up moment, not a shopping trigger.
