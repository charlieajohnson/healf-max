# Category role

B12 support is a diet-context lane for plant-based, vegetarian or vegan customers, especially when fatigue or borderline bloods are part of the story.

# Fit

Use this category to explain why B12 is worth checking when animal-food intake is low. If the result is low, borderline with symptoms, or neurological symptoms are present, route to clinical review before product certainty.

# Boundary

Do not frame B12 as a stimulant, energy hack or cure for tiredness.
