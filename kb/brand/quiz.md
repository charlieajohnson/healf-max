# Healf quiz — profiling flow and tone

- AI-powered, with an explicit disclaimer: "This quiz is AI-powered and may contain mistakes. This is not medical advice and should not replace professional guidance."
- Framing: "Let's find what products work for your wellbeing."
- Profiling sequence observed: first name (skippable) -> sex (Male / Female / Prefer not to say) -> age band (18-24 ... 65+) -> wellbeing goals -> recommendations.
- Copy voice: warm, playful, personal ("Age is just a number, what's yours?").

Assistant implication: assemble context from sex + age + goal (plus biomarker/wearable when present); keep copy warm and personal; carry the AI and not-medical-advice disclaimer lightly, the way Healf already does.
