# Brand essence

- Mission: "empower wellbeing — whatever that looks like for you."
- The Four Pillars (trademarked): true wellbeing emerges from the balance and synergy of EAT, MOVE, MIND, SLEEP. Everything files under a pillar.
- Founders: two brothers, Max and Lestat, with "one vision: to be well", founded from their own health journey. UK. FT1000 number one fastest-growing company in Europe.
- Curation process (why customers trust it): 01 Brand Discovery (global network of health professionals plus AI-powered trend analysis) -> 02 Expert Validation -> 03 Community Testing. Plus a banned-ingredients standard.
- Mechanics/proof points: free UK delivery over £50, 365-day returns, buy 3 get 1 free, subscriptions, in-house practitioners.
