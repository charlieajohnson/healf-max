# Signal

HYROX is a hybrid race: repeated running plus functional stations under fatigue. The customer needs enough fuel, enough recovery and enough consistency before they need a complicated stack.

# Use

When a customer is training for Hyrox and feeling tired, start with the bottleneck: sleep quality, protein/fuelling consistency, hydration and iron/B12 context when diet or bloods suggest it.

# Commercial boundary

Good lanes are protein for repair, creatine for strength stations, electrolytes for longer efforts, magnesium for sleep fit, and iron only when bloods and professional guidance support it. Do not say a product makes someone race-ready.
