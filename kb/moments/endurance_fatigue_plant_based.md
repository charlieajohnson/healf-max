# Summary

This moment appears when a plant-based endurance or Hyrox customer is tired, sleeping poorly and trying to keep the stack low-chaos. The honest read is not "more products". It is iron/B12 context, sleep pressure and steady fuelling.

# Answer shape

Lead with the bottleneck: iron stores, B12 risk from diet, low deep sleep and training load are pointing in the same direction. Recommend bloods or follow-up where markers are low or borderline, then offer two or three category lanes at most.

# Useful lanes

Iron support only belongs after bloods and appropriate guidance. B12 is worth checking because of the diet pattern. Protein and electrolytes support training consistency. Magnesium can be a sleep-support lane if medication and kidney context are safe.

# Boundary

Respect caffeine sensitivity. Do not recommend stimulant energy products, and do not turn low ferritin or borderline B12 into a supplement shopping list.
