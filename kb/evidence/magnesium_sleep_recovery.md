Magnesium may support relaxation, nervous-system regulation and sleep-routine consistency for some customers. It should be framed as sleep-routine support, not a promise of improved wearable scores.

# Use when

Useful for low deep sleep, recovery pressure and low-chaos evening routines. Keep wording modest and category-first.
