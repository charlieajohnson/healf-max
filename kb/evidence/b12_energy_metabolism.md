# Summary

B12 is relevant to energy metabolism and is especially worth checking in mostly plant-based diets.

# Use when

If B12 is borderline or low, explain the signal and route to follow-up before product certainty. Avoid promising tiredness resolution.

# Useful phrasing

Start with the bottleneck and explain the signal without turning the answer into a stack.

# Boundary

This record supports wellness education and category routing only. It does not establish diagnosis, treatment or dosing.
