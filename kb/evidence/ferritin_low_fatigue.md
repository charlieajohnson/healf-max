# Summary

Low ferritin can be relevant to tiredness, training tolerance and recovery. It should trigger follow-up language and plain-English explanation before any product lane is discussed.

# Use when

Use this when tiredness appears alongside low ferritin, heavy training, mostly plant-based diet, or poor recovery. The answer should avoid anaemia diagnosis and avoid iron dosing.

# Useful phrasing

Start with the bottleneck and explain the signal without turning the answer into a stack.

# Boundary

This record supports wellness education and category routing only. It does not establish diagnosis, treatment or dosing.
