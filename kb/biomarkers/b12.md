# Summary

B12 is relevant in mostly plant-based diets, red blood cell formation, energy metabolism and nervous-system function. Borderline or low status should be explained and followed up rather than over-claimed.

# Plain-language route

Explain B12 as the energy-and-nerves vitamin. For a plant-based customer with persistent fatigue, B12 is a high-value thing to check because intake is often lower when animal foods are limited.

# Symptom boundary

If the customer mentions numbness, tingling, balance change, memory change or other neurological symptoms, recommend clinical review before product advice.

# Product boundary

B12 support is a diet-context lane. Do not frame it as an energy boost or a tiredness cure.
